Rukiruki punipuni a hūrokuroku whare tapere
A PS4 papatohu, puaki ka mau te wehi punipuni taura hiko, rukiruki whare tapere a ngangahau HDR hangarau.

Ētahi atu huarahi ko hākinakina
Whakaputu tō punipuni, pūmanawa tautono, kapomata a ataata me tīemiemi ko 1TB putunga roto te PS4 papatohu – rahirahi apūahi āhaku te orokohanga PS4 tauira a wāteatanga pēraka torohaki pango a atu muramura.

Hīanga te reira i runga PlayStation
Whakapukepuke ngō pēraka ko a toa Royale, wini te ao tūroa raupapatanga a kaea ngō kapa rau putakari. Me ehinu o te nui rawa a tino punipuni wāteatanga i runga PS4 a miriona o kaitākaro ao whānui, katoa neki tatari mo koe.


PlayStation VR punipuni
Kaiao te punipuni i roto inati hou huarahi me PS4 a PlayStation VR. Maama noa raka te PS VR whakapiringa māhunga ko tāu papatohu a whakatotohu koe i roto whakahihiri virtual-reality punipuni, tūmahi wheako a whare tapere.
